Detailed Description

One KS0108 can control 64x64 pixels. Therefore two chips are needed for a normal 128x64 pixel display. One chip controls the left half, the other the right half (see PIN_CS1 and PIN_CS2).

Template Parameters:
EEnable Pin
RWRead/Write-Select Pin (Low = write, High = read)
RSCommand/Data-Select Pin (Low = instruction, High = data)
PIN_CS1Chip-Select 1
PIN_CS2Chip-Select 2
PORTNeeds to be a 8-Bit Port. See xpcc::gpio::Port() or GPIO__PORT() for more details.
